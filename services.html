Our Process

Get Fitted

Phil K Men’s Bepoke Clothing offers made-to-measure clothing. Our stylists will measure you with precision to ensure a perfect.

There is no pattern, unless we use your unique pattern to fashion additional clothing for you at a later time. Imagine the beauty and comfort of clothing made to fit you perfectly; suits, dress pants, dress shirts, and outerwear.

Choose Your Style

Our clothiers will help you choose your new look by curating from a massive collection of styles and fabrics from the best mills in Italy, including Zegna, Loro Piana, Barberis, and more.

Get The Finished Product

Your custom garments will be ready in 4-6 weeks from purchase. Your clothier will meet you to deliver the garment and insure it fits great and your 100% satisfied. Occasionally a 2nd fitting is necessary with minor adjustments to your garment to get the perfect fit.
