Schedule An Appointment

We will set an appointment that fits with your busy schedule. We understand that your time is valuable and we will make this process as seamless as possible.

Our stylists will schedule a convenient time with you and come to your office for a fitting and wardrobe appointment. If your office schedule is too hectic, we will make arrangements to come to your home to do the fitting. Our team of stylists is available to work after hours and on weekends to meet so will find a time that works for you.


Get Fitted

Phil K Men’s Bepoke Clothing offers made-to-measure clothing. Our stylists will measure you with extreme precision to ensure a perfect fit.

There is no cookie-cutter process to fitting, unless we use your unique pattern to fashion additional clothing for you at a later time. We will leave no stone unturned when it comes to getting your measurements. Imagine the beauty and comfort of clothing made to fit you perfectly; suits, dress pants, dress shirts, and outerwear.


Choose Your Style

Our clothiers will help you choose your new look by curating from a massive collection of styles and fabrics from the best mills in Italy, including Zegna, Loro Piana, Barberis, and more.

Our clothiers will assist you in picking out the fabrics and styles that best suit you.


Get The Finished Product

Your custom garments will be ready in 4-6 weeks from purchase. Your clothier will meet you to deliver the garment and ensure that they fit properly. Our goal is to make sure that our customers are 100% satisfied so occasionally a 2nd fitting with minor adjustments to your garments is necessary to get the perfect fit.

Who We Are

Phil K Men’s Bespoke Clothing company is a custom clothier based out of New Jersey. Our mission is to help our customers look great, with personalized and fitted garments, as conveniently as possible. We do not use any cookie-cutter patterns. We are not a factory and we do not follow factory practices. Each piece is designed to your specifications to suit your individuality. Our company offers some of the best fabrics available from the top Italian mills so you can be sure that you're receiving high quality. We know that our full line of custom clothing (including dress, formal, and casual menswear) will exceed your expectations.
