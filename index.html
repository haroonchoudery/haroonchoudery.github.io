Who We Are

Phil K Men’s Bespoke Clothing company is a custom clothier based out of New Jersey. Our mission is to help our customers look great, with personalized and fitted garments, as conveniently as possible. We do not use any cookie-cutter patterns. We are not a factory and we do not follow factory practices. Each piece is designed to your specifications to suit your individuality. Our company offers some of the best fabrics available from the top Italian mills so you can be sure that you're receiving high quality. We know that our full line of custom clothing (including dress, formal, and casual menswear) will exceed your expectations.
