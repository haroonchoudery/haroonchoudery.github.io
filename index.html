About Me

I am a currently enrolled college student with special interests in the fields of Economics and Technology.

Having such an extraordinarily diverse set of academic interests allow me to have a very unique perspective in both the world of technology as well as the world of economics. My goals is to utilize my skills in both of these fields in a synergistic manner.
