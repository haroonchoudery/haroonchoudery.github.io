What is aNYCdote?

Simply put, aNYCdote is a tool that allows New Yorkers to connect with one-another in a way never done before. It is a platform where New Yorkers can listen to the stories of other New Yorkers, or share their own. The infinite stories are what makes New York the best city in the world. We just wanted to provide a way for New Yorkers to share them.

Our Mission

We want to make New York more personal than ever. Every day, hundreds of thousands of New Yorkers roam the streets, passing by flocks of strangers. These stangers have countless stories to tell - some goofy, some exhilerating, and some heart-breaking. If us New Yorkers were given a chance to listen to the stories of the strangers around them, we could see these flocks of strangers in a much more personal way.
